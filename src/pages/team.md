## Research Group

This is an undergraduate research project in the School of Information Technology at Illinois State University. It was proposed by [Dr. Mangolika Bhattacharya](https://it.illinoisstate.edu/faculty-staff/profile/?ulid=mbhatt1), who advises the group, and is carried out by three undergraduate researchers. [Dr. Sally Xie](https://tec.illinoisstate.edu/faculty-staff/profile/?ulid=hxie) contributes to the project's guidance as well.

The project explores how virtual reality can support building simulation and CAD/BIM-based education, building on models authored in tools such as Autodesk Revit and made interactive for inspection and learning. Each researcher leads a primary area, described on the [research areas](/research/) page, while the work itself stays collaborative.

## Researchers

- **Damian Bednarczyk** — simulation development
- **Miguel Macias** — CAD and building data workflow
- **Abraham Gallardo** — sustainability, documentation, and adoption research

These roles are just primary contact areas for specific research areas. The overall project work is largely collaborative: each member helps out with each task.
